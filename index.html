Bio

As an undergrad at Boston University studying Data Science and Health Science, I am also pursuing an MPH at BU's School of Public Health with certificates in Epidemiology and Biostatistics and in Pharmaceutical Development, Delivery, and Access.

I chose this combination because the problems I'm drawn to don't fit neatly into one field. Data science is how I do the work, and public health is why it matters to me, since it keeps me focused on who a system is actually serving and who it's leaving out. Long term, I want to work in pharmaceutical tech or healthcare administration, using analytics to close the gap between what the data already shows and what actually reaches patients.
